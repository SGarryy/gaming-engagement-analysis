# Gaming User Segmentation Report
**Date:** March 6, 2026
**Objective:** Identify distinct player behaviors to optimize server allocation and retention marketing.

## Key Findings
1. **Cluster 0 (Casuals):** 45% of users. Low frequency, low duration. Recommendation: Use "Welcome Back" push notifications.
2. **Cluster 1 (Power Users):** 15% of users. High frequency, high duration. Recommendation: Priority server bandwidth and loyalty rewards.
3. **Cluster 2 (The Grinders):** 20% of users. Moderate frequency, high milestone completion. Recommendation: Introduce "Elite" tier challenges.
4. **Cluster 3 (At-Risk):** 20% of users. Declining engagement patterns. Recommendation: Churn prevention email campaigns.
